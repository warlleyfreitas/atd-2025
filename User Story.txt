Title: Checkout with Valid Payment Methods

As a registered user,
I want to complete a checkout using an accepted payment method,
so that I can purchase products from the store.

Description

- The system must allow authenticated users to perform checkout for one or more products.
- Payment can be made using either cash or credit card.
- Depending on the payment method, specific business rules apply.

Rules

- Payment Methods: Only cash and credit card are accepted
- Cash Discount: Cash payments receive a 10% discount
- Credit Card: No discounts are applied
- Authentication: Only authenticated users can perform checkout
- Product Validation: Products must exist and have sufficient stock
